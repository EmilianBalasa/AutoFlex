Frequently Asked Questions

How can I become a service provider on Autoflex?

To become a service provider on Autoflex, click on "Register Your Business" on our homepage. You'll need to complete our application form, provide business documentation, and pass our quality verification process. Our team will contact you within 2-3 business days to complete the onboarding process.

How do I schedule an appointment with a service provider?

You can schedule an appointment by first searching for your desired service and location on our homepage. Browse the available providers, select one that fits your needs, and click "Book Appointment." Choose your preferred date and time, confirm your selection, and you're all set! You'll receive a confirmation email with all the details.

What payment methods do you accept?

Autoflex accepts all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, and Apple Pay. Your payment is secure and processed only after the service has been completed to your satisfaction. Some service providers may also accept cash payments directly.

Can I cancel or reschedule my appointment?

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule your appointment through your Autoflex account. Go to "My Appointments" and select the appointment you wish to modify. You can cancel for free up to 24 hours before your scheduled time. Late cancellations may incur a fee depending on the provider's policy.
